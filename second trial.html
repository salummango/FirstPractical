In this field we will be discussing about various issues such as economic issues,social issues and political issues. All those issues will be in mode of production that consist three modes which are

  1. PRIMITIVE COMMUNALISM
  2. SLAVERY
  3. FEUDALISM

PRIMITIVE COMMUNALISM

This is the first socio-economic mode of production in which evolution of the society started.it covered the period of STONES,BRONZE and IRON AGES

CHARECTERISTICS OF PRIMITIVE COMMUNALISM

LOW LEVEL OF PRODUCTION
with crude tools and little knowledge, man hardly mastered his surroundings

EQUALITY TO ALL MEMBERS
communial societies were not stratified

SLAVERY

This is the second mode of production on which for the first time antagonism in human history evolved.Also in this mode exploitation,suppression and humiliation of human being started to take place.

CHARACTERISTIC OF SLAVERY

  1. HUMILIATION
    many people started to humiliate each other in various issues

  2. EXPLOITATION
    many people tarted to exploit each other

FEUDALISM

This was the third mode of production that also involve extreme exploitation of man by man.Also this mode was based on LAND and PROPERTY
CHARACTERISTIC OF FEUDALISM

  • LABOUR RENT
    labour rent was also termed as land rent and had two dimension.

  • MONEY RENT
    This was the last form of feudal rent that grew in popularity after money became a majour medium of exchange.
